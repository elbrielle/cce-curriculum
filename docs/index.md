# CCE Curriculum — Bowie Middle School

**Career and College Explorations** | Irving ISD | TEKS §127.2

A 36-week career exploration course for grades 7-8. Each week has a **Weekly Overview** (objectives, TEKS, materials, assessments) and **5 Daily Lesson Plans** (detailed activity flows with deliverables).

## Platforms

| Platform | Role |
|----------|------|
| **Hats & Ladders** | Core: RIASEC, career clusters, Hat Finder, Career Plan, workbook activities |
| **Xello** | Supplement: quizzes, career saves, resume, course planning |
| **eDynamic Learning** | Supplement: career exploration units |
| **VILS Tech** | Hands-on: Sphero, Glowforge, drones, micro:bit, TinkerCAD |

## Prototype

This site currently contains the **5th Six Weeks, Week 1: Architecture** as a fully developed prototype. The remaining 35 weeks will follow the same format.
