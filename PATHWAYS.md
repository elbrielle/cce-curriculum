## How to cite pathways in week overviews

1. **Use the exact campus name.** "Real Estate at MacArthur High School" is more useful than "Real Estate is an Irving ISD pathway."
2. **Do not over-claim credentials.** If the campus pathway doesn't explicitly confer a specific certification on the website, don't name one. "Prepares students for [field]" is safer than "leads to [specific credential] certification."
3. **For multi-campus pathways** (Automotive, Cosmetology, Computer Science, etc.) list all campuses or name the "district-wide / multi-campus" framing explicitly.
4. **For week topics without an Irving ISD home** (HVAC, Medical Billing), frame the week as career exploration using BLS + O*NET + CareerOneStop, not as a claim about a local pathway.
5. **When in doubt, verify with the Irving ISD CTE Offerings by Campus page** before citing a pathway. Do not rely on this file alone.
